Writing
a good paper, I think, is one of life's supreme joys. Unfettered, My mind will swell with excitement in the midst of a whirlwind of thoughts and ideas. Likewise, however, rereading that paper again is one of life's greatest agitators. Every other sentence, I cannot help wincing at my own pompousness. Though at odds, these two experiences are forever inseperable,
and nor should they be!
I can't speak for anyone else, but personally, they represent what I think are my two best qualities: on the one hand, a grand optimism and excitement from and through challenge; and on the other, a realistic, critical edge to mediate with. Both are vitally important for mantaining a healthy outlook (Life's other supreme joys, if you were wondering, are
cool stationery and yellow beanies).
